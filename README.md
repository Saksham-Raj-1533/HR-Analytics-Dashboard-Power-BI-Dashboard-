# HR-Analytics-Dashboard-Power-BI

Interactive HR Analytics Dashboard built in Power BI to analyse employee attrition, workforce demographics, and key HR metrics through dynamic visualisations and drill-down analysis.

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive HR Analytics Dashboard built in Power BI to analyse employee attrition patterns and workforce trends. The dashboard tracks key HR metrics, including employee count, attrition, attrition rate, average age, average salary, and average years at the company. It enables dynamic analysis across departments, job roles, education levels, age groups, and tenure to identify major attrition drivers and support data-driven HR decision-making.

## Key Insights

- Analysed workforce data of 1,470 employees with 237 attritions, resulting in an attrition rate of 16.1%.
- Identified compensation as a major attrition driver, with 69% of employees who left earning below ₹5K per month.
- Found that employees aged 26–35 accounted for 49% of total attrition.
- Observed the highest attrition during the first year of employment, indicating potential onboarding and retention challenges.
- Analysed attrition across departments, job roles, education fields, and tenure using interactive filters and drill-down capabilities.
